Coordinator: Deb Olson 

Program Description:

All qualified candidates for the 2026 U.S. House of Representatives Primary, Washington's 5th Congressional District have been invited to participate in this candidate forum.

The forum is open to all qualified candidates. Invitations were extended to every qualified candidate, and participation is voluntary.  As of July 12th, the candidates in bold on the list below have confirmed that they will attend the forum.

Questions for the candidates must be submitted by July 12 by emailing [lwvpull@yahoo.com](mailto:lwvpull@yahoo.com).

Date and Time: Monday, July 13th at 6:30 pm

Place:  Pullman Recreation Center, Room 100, 
190 SE Crestview, Building B, next to City Hall, Room 100 is on the lower level next to gym and Senior Center.

Candidates for the 2026 U.S. House of Representatives Primary, Washington's 5th Congressional District are as follows:

Michael Baumgartner (Incumbent - R)

**Andrew Bartleson (I)

Carmela Conroy (D)**

Ann Marie Danimus (I)

**Kevin Fagan (D)

Richard Freudenberg (D)

Matthew Hayes (I)

Bajun R Malalwalla (D)**

Michael McGarr (D)

**Nate Powell (I)**

Kyle Ursey(I)

**David Womack (D)**


The forum will be recorded and available for viewing here and on our YouTube Channel within 24 hours of the forum.

Ballots will be mailed starting on July 17th. Election Day is August 4th. We recommend using an official ballot drop box. If you choose to mail your ballot, mail it at least 10 days before Election Day. Please make sure you sign your ballot.
