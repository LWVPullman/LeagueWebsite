# Where Civics Meets Art

**Speakers:**

Visual Arts Committee Members and Desiree Greystone, artist/owner of Greystone Gallery

**Program:** 

Calling all students and families - Come learn about local government and a creative way to explore civics and make your voice heard through art.  This competition is for all students in grades 3 to 12 living in Whitman County, WA. Students will have until March 30th to enter their artwork.  [Check out the competition webpage.](https://lwvpullman.org/docs/art%20competition/civics%20education/Visual_Arts_Competition_26/)

More information coming soon.

**Coordinators:** Fran Powers and Desiree Greystone

**Date:** March 4, 2026

**Time:** 6:00-7:30 pm

**Location:** Greystone Art Gallery, 245 S Grand Ave, Pullman
