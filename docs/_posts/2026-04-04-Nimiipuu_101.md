# Nez Perce Education in Idaho and Washington State, Tribal Sovereignty, and Treaties

**Program:**

The role of the Nez Perce Tribe’s Education Department is to promote the well being of the Nimiipuu people. Achieving this has taken different forms in Idaho and Washington State. Joyce McFarland, Nez Perce education manager, will discuss the status of tribal education. Jeanette Moody, Nez Perce attorney, will discuss Tribal Sovereignty and Treaties.

To learn more about the tribe’s decisions and initiatives, the League of Women Voters of Pullman invites you to meet with program representatives and hear directly about their work.

**Speakers:**

* **Joyce McFarland** - Nez Perce Education Manager

* **Jeanette Moody** - Nez Perce Attorney

**Coordinator:** Theresa Jordan

**Date:** April 4, 2026

**Time:** 10:30 am

**Location:** Neill Public Library, Pullman
